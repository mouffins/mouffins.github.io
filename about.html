Hey there! I'm Shelly, or Fantastic Sky and mouffins, depending on where you find me. For the sake of brevity, I'll introduce myself as mouffins.

Perhaps I'm known as a fervent music advocate, who happens to enjoy crocheting on the side; or a feeble one to the spiciest of foods, like those seasoned with black pepper; or an instructor/officer of varying flavors, trying to make the world a better place; or an incoming undergraduate at MIT, if that happens to be relevant for some reason. But I'm also an individual video producer, armed with an iPhone 12 mini, Adobe Premiere Pro, and a ton of ambition; or a STEM enthusiast eager to figure out how people work through a combination of math and neuroscience; or as you may meet me here, a blogger who loves to overthink and yap.

I will be posting essays, rants, and all the different varieties of word soup here, so if that’s your jam, welcome!
