Hey there! I'm Fantastic Sky, Muffin_Potato, or mouffin depending on where you find me. For the sake of brevity, I'll introduce myself as mouffin.

Perhaps I'm known as a fervent boba milk tea advocate, who happens to enjoy crocheting on the side; or a feeble one to the spiciest of foods, like those seasoned with black pepper; or an instructor/officer of varying flavors, trying to make the world a better place. But I'm also an individual video producer, armed with an iPhone 12 mini, Adobe Premiere Pro, and a ton of ambition; or a STEM enthusiast eager to figure out how people work through a combination of math and biology; or as you may meet me here, a blogger who loves to overthink and yap.

I will be posting essays, rants, and all the different varieties of word soup here, so if that’s your jam, welcome!
